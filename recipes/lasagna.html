Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a delicious dish that is enjoyed by many around the world. Having originated in ancient Rome before being adopted by the Italians, every family--and restaurant!--has their own special take on the dish.

Today, I'll be walking you through Danilo Alfaro's lasagna recipe. Their recipe employs both red and white sauce, ensuring that there is peace at the dinner table when it turns out that Joey doesn't like eating white sauce anymore!

Ingredients

For The White Sauce:

  • 4 tbs butter
  • 2 1/2 cups whole milk
  • 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt

For The Red Sauce:

  • 2 tbs olive oil
  • 1/2 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 pound ground pork
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 6 to 8 white mushrooms, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 28-ounce cans tomato sauce
  • 2 tsps dried oregano
  • 2 tsps dried basil
  • kosher salt, to taste
  • black pepper, to taste

For Assembling:

  • 1 package oven-ready lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

For The White Sauce:

  1. Melt the butter in a heavy-bottomed saucepan.
  2. Warm milk in another saucepan. Do not let it boil.
  3. Add flour to melted butter and stir to form a roux. Cook for about two minutes.
  4. Slowly whisk in warm milk. Season with kosher salt.
  5. Turn off heat and reserve, covered.

For the Red Sauce:

  1. In a large skillet, heat up olive oil and add ground beef and pork.
  2. Cook until all meat is well-browned, approximately 8 minutes.
  3. Drain excess fat from meat and reserve 2 tbs in skillet. Remove meat mixture from pan. Cover and set aside.
  4. Add onion to skillet with reserved fat and saut√© until translucent, about 6-7 minutes.
  5. Add mushrooms and garlic to skillet. Cook until mushrooms are soft, or 5 minutes.
  6. Return meat to pan and stir well.
  7. Add tomato sauce and dried herbs.
  8. Bring meat sauce to simmer and cook for another 5 minutes. Remove red sauce from heat and season to taste with salt and pepper.

Assemble the Lasagna:

  1. Preheat oven to 400F. In 3-quart ovenproof baking dish, ladle enough red sauce to cover bottom of dish.
  2. Arrange layer of pasta atop red meat sauce, then add red sauce.
  3. Drizzle about 1/3 of white sauce even atop red sauce.
  4. Repeat previous step twice by layering layers of pasta, red sauce, white sauce.
  5. Spread shredded mozzarella on top of final white sauce layer.
  6. Sprinkle grated parmesan on top of mozzarella.
  7. Cover dish tightly with foil and bake for 40 minutes.
  8. After 40 minutes, remove foil and bake for about 10 more minutes, or until cheese on top has browned slightly. Remove from oven and let rest for 10 minutes.
  9. Enjoy!
